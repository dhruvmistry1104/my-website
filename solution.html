Solutions For SDG 4

Local Solutions in India

  • Improve government school infrastructure.
  • Provide digital learning tools and internet access.
  • Train teachers in modern teaching methods.
  • Support free education programs for low-income families.

Global Solutions

  • Increase funding for education worldwide.
  • Support education for girls and marginalized communities.
  • Provide technology-based learning opportunities.
  • Promote lifelong learning programs.
